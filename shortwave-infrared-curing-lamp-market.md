<p><p>The Shortwave Infrared Curing Lamp market is segmented based on the orientation of the curing process: lengthwise direction and lateral direction. Lengthwise direction refers to lamps that cure materials in a straight-line manner, ideal for continuous processes or elongated surfaces. In contrast, lateral direction lamps distribute heat across a wider area, targeting broader or irregularly shaped objects. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and precision in applications like coating, printing, and material bonding.</p></p>
