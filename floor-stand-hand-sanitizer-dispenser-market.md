<p><p>The floor stand hand sanitizer dispenser market can be categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact dispensers. Contact dispensers require users to push a button or lever to dispense sanitizer, facilitating manual operation. In contrast, non-contact dispensers utilize infrared sensors to release sanitizer automatically when hands are detected nearby, promoting a touch-free experience. This distinction caters to varying consumer preferences, with non-contact options becoming increasingly popular for their enhanced hygiene and convenience in public spaces.</p></p>
