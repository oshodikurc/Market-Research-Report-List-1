<p><p>The Medical Gas Pipeline System market is primarily divided into two types: centralized and distributed systems. The centralized type involves a main source of medical gases, such as oxygen or nitrous oxide, typically located in a central area, supplying multiple points in a healthcare facility through a network of pipes. Conversely, the distributed type features multiple gas distribution points, often closer to clinical areas, allowing for more localized access to gases, improving responsiveness and efficiency in patient care delivery.</p></p>
