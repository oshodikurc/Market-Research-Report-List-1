<p><p>The Employee Performance Software market can be categorized into three main types: on-premise, cloud-based, and web-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on company servers, offering greater control but requiring maintenance and upfront costs. Cloud-based software is hosted remotely, providing scalability, automatic updates, and remote access, ideal for distributed teams. Web-based solutions combine features of both, accessible via browsers without installations, facilitating ease of use and integration with other cloud services, enhancing collaboration and performance tracking.</p></p>
