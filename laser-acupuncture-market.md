<p><strong>The Laser Acupuncture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbon source</li><li>He-Ne light source</li><li>Semiconductor laser</li><li>Nd: YAG laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser acupuncture market includes various types of laser sources, each with unique characteristics. The carbon source provides a continuous wave that penetrates tissues effectively. He-Ne light sources emit low-level red light, ideal for superficial applications. Semiconductor lasers offer compactness and versatility, producing specific wavelengths for targeted therapies. Nd:YAG lasers deliver high-intensity energy suitable for deeper tissue penetration, providing efficient treatment options for pain relief and tissue regeneration. Each type caters to different therapeutic needs in acupuncture practices.</p></p>
