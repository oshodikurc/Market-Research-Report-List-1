Toremifene, also a SERM, serves as an alternative to Tamoxifen for hormone receptor-positive cases. Fulvestrant, an estrogen receptor down-regulator (SERD), works by degrading the receptor, leading to a decrease in tumor activity. These therapies are crucial for managing hormone-receptor-positive breast cancer, offering tailored treatment options that improve patient outcomes and survival rates.</p></p>
