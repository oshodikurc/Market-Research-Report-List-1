<p><p>Solid state detectors are pivotal in various applications for detecting ionizing radiation. Surface-barrier detectors utilize thin layers of semiconductor material for efficient energy resolution. Silicon drift detectors feature a unique design that enhances detection efficiency by minimizing charge carrier movement. Lithium-drifted silicon detectors, infused with lithium, provide improved energy resolution for X-ray and gamma-ray detection. Intrinsic detectors, made from high-purity semiconductors, operate at low temperatures for superior sensitivity. Together, these technologies serve distinct roles in ionizing radiation detection across diverse fields.</p></p>
