<p><strong>The Auto Parts Cleaner and Degreaser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto parts cleaner and degreaser market is divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based cleaners. Water-based cleaners utilize water as the primary solvent, offering a safer and environmentally friendly option with less volatile organic compounds (VOCs). They are effective for light to moderate cleaning tasks. In contrast, solvent-based cleaners contain organic solvents that provide strong degreasing power, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. However, they can pose health risks and environmental concerns due to higher VOC content.</p></p>
