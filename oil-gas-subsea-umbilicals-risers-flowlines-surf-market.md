<p><p>The Oil & Gas Subsea Umbilicals, Risers, and Flowlines (SURF) market comprises three main components: umbilicals, risers, and flowlines. Umbilicals are essential for supplying fluids and control signals from surface facilities to subsea equipment. Risers provide a conduit for transporting hydrocarbons from the seabed to surface platforms, while flowlines are used for transporting produced fluids between subsea wells and processing facilities. Together, these elements enable efficient subsea operations, reducing costs and enhancing production capabilities in offshore environments.</p></p>
