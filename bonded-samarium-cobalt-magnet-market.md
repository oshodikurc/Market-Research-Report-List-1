<p><p>The bonded samarium cobalt magnet market primarily consists of two types: SmCo₅ and Sm₂Co₁. SmCo₅ magnets offer high magnetic strength and excellent thermal stability, making them suitable for high-performance applications like motors and sensors. In contrast, Sm₂Co₁ magnets provide enhanced resistance to demagnetization and are often used in challenging environments. Both types are known for their corrosion resistance and maintain their magnetic properties at elevated temperatures, driving demand across various industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
