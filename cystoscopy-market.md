<p><ul><li>Rigid Cystoscope</li><li>Flexible Cystoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cystoscopy market is divided into two main types: rigid cystoscopes and flexible cystoscopes. Rigid cystoscopes are straight, sturdy instruments ideal for surgical procedures, providing clear visuals of the bladder lining but may cause discomfort during insertion. In contrast, flexible cystoscopes are designed with a malleable structure, allowing easier navigation and patient comfort, making them suitable for diagnostics and biopsies. Both types are essential in urology for diagnosing and treating various bladder conditions, with distinct advantages based on the procedure.</p></p>
