<p><p>The diagnostic catheters market encompasses various specialized catheters used in medical imaging and electrophysiological procedures. Angiography catheters are designed for vascular imaging, allowing physicians to visualize blood vessels and diagnose conditions like blockages or stenosis. Electrophysiology (EP) catheters, on the other hand, are used to assess and treat heart arrhythmias by mapping electrical activity within the heart. Both catheter types are essential for accurate diagnosis and effective treatment in cardiovascular care.</p></p>
