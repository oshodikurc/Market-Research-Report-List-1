<p><ul><li>Gravity Suction Type</li><li>Direct Pressure Type</li><li>Blower Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air blasting machine market is categorized into three main types: Gravity Suction Type, Direct Pressure Type, and Blower Type. The Gravity Suction Type utilizes gravity to draw abrasive material, providing a low-cost option for light applications. The Direct Pressure Type delivers a constant flow of abrasive under pressure, ideal for heavy-duty tasks. The Blower Type employs high-velocity air to propel abrasives, suitable for industrial applications requiring rapid surface cleaning and finishing. Each type offers distinct advantages based on operational requirements.</p></p>
