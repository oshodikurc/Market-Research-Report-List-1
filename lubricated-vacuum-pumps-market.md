<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multi Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lubricated vacuum pumps are categorized into single-stage and multi-stage types based on their design and operational efficiency. Single-stage pumps utilize one set of rotors to create a vacuum, making them suitable for applications requiring moderate vacuum levels. In contrast, multi-stage pumps consist of multiple rotor sets, enabling them to achieve deeper vacuums and higher efficiency, ideal for industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing. These distinctions determine the appropriate usage based on specific vacuum requirements and operational demands.</p></p>
