<p><p>The trolley jack market is segmented into hydraulic jacks, pneumatic jacks, and other types. Hydraulic jacks, the most common, use fluid pressure for lifting and are favored for their robustness and ease of use. Pneumatic jacks leverage compressed air, offering rapid lifting capabilities and are ideal for high-volume applications. Other types may include electric jacks and scissor jacks, each catering to specific needs. The choice between these options depends on factors like lifting capacity, speed, and application requirements.</p></p>
