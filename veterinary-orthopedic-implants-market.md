<p><p>The veterinary orthopedic implants market encompasses various types designed to stabilize and repair animal bone fractures and joint issues. Plates provide stable fixation for complex bone fractures, while screws secure bone segments together. Pins and wires assist in alignment and stabilization during healing. Staples are used for soft tissue fixation. Joint replacement implants, including artificial joints, offer solutions for severe degenerative conditions, restoring mobility and quality of life in animals. Each type serves specific orthopedic challenges in veterinary medicine.</p></p>
