<p><p>Warehouse Control Systems (WCS) can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise systems are installed locally within a warehouse, providing greater control and customization over operations but requiring significant hardware and maintenance investments. Cloud-based systems, on the other hand, are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced upfront costs. They allow for easy access and real-time updates, making them ideal for businesses looking to streamline their operations and adapt to changing needs.</p></p>
