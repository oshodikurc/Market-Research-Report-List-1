<p><p>Medical Grade Polyurethane (PU) encompasses various forms, primarily including foams, elastomers, films, and others. Foams are used for cushioning, wound dressings, and prosthetics due to their softness and breathability. Elastomers offer flexibility and durability, making them suitable for applications like medical tubing and seals. Films provide a barrier against moisture and bacteria, often used in surgical drapes and packaging. Other forms may include coatings and adhesives, expanding the scope of PU's application in medical devices and healthcare products.</p></p>
