<p><p>The discrete and power lead frames market comprises various package types for semiconductor devices. Dual In-line Package (DIP) features two rows of pins and is widely used for through-hole applications. The Small Outline Package (SOP) and Small Outline Transistor (SOT) provide compact surface-mount solutions. Quad Flat Package (QFP) offers a high pin count in a flat format, while Dual Flat No-lead (DFN) and Quad Flat No-lead (QFN) enhance thermal performance. The TO package is often used for transistors and other power components, along with other specialized forms.</p></p>
