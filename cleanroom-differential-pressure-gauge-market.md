<p><ul><li>Mechanical Differential Pressure Gauge</li><li>Digital Differential Pressure Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cleanroom differential pressure gauge market is segmented into two primary types: mechanical and digital differential pressure gauges. Mechanical differential pressure gauges utilize traditional analog methods, employing spring mechanisms to measure pressure differences, making them simple and reliable for basic applications. In contrast, digital differential pressure gauges provide precise readings through electronic sensors, often featuring advanced functionalities such as data logging and remote monitoring. Both types are essential for maintaining the required pressure levels in cleanroom environments, ensuring contamination control.</p></p>
