<p><p>The climbing and falling film plate evaporator market is segmented into single-effect and multiple-effect systems. Single-effect evaporators utilize a single stage for evaporation, making them simpler and suitable for less concentrated solutions. Multiple-effect evaporators, on the other hand, operate in stages, allowing for more efficient heat use and higher concentration levels in a given footprint. This efficiency makes multiple-effect systems preferred in industries seeking to minimize energy consumption and maximize output, driving their demand in the market.</p></p>
