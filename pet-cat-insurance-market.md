<p><strong>The Pet Cat Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lifetime Cover</li><li>Non-lifetime Cover</li><li>Accident-only</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pet cat insurance comes in three main market types. Lifetime cover provides continuous coverage for chronic conditions throughout a cat’s life, ensuring expenses are covered as long as premiums are paid. Non-lifetime cover limits coverage to a specified period or up to a maximum payout, ending once claims for a condition are reached. Accident-only insurance focuses solely on injuries from accidents, excluding illnesses and routine care, offering a lower-premium option for those primarily concerned with unexpected accidents.</p></p>
