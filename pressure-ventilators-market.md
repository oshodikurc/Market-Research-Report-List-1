<p><p>The pressure ventilators market comprises two main types: positive pressure and negative pressure ventilators. Positive pressure ventilators deliver air into the lungs, effectively aiding patients with respiratory issues by increasing lung volume and enhancing oxygenation. In contrast, negative pressure ventilators create a lower pressure environment around the thoracic cavity, facilitating inhalation by drawing air into the lungs naturally. Both types serve critical roles in respiratory support, catering to diverse medical needs in hospital and home care settings.</p></p>
