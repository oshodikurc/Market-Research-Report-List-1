<p><strong>The Hydrogen Peroxide Disinfection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vaporized Type</li><li>Fogging Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hydrogen Peroxide Disinfection System market features two primary types: Vaporized and Fogging. Vaporized systems generate a fine mist of hydrogen peroxide vapor, effectively sterilizing surfaces and air in enclosed spaces, making them suitable for hospitals and laboratories. Fogging systems, on the other hand, disperse a liquid aerosol of hydrogen peroxide, covering larger areas and enabling efficient disinfection of both surfaces and equipment. Both methods are increasingly adopted for their efficacy in reducing pathogens and ensuring safe environments.</p></p>
