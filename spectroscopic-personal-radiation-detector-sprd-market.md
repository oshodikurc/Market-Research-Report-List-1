<p><p>Spectroscopic Personal Radiation Detectors (SPRDs) are advanced devices used for identifying and measuring radiation levels. The market for SPRDs can be categorized based on Gas Ionization, Scintillator, and Semiconductor technologies. </p><p>Gas ionization SPRDs leverage ionized gas to detect radiation, while scintillator SPRDs utilize luminescent materials that emit light upon radiation interaction. Semiconductor SPRDs employ solid-state detectors, providing high sensitivity and resolution. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to diverse applications in radiation safety and monitoring.</p></p>
