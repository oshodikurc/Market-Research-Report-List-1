<p><p>The laboratory mills market comprises various types, including ball mills, disc mills, rotor mills, cutting mills, and others. Ball mills utilize spherical grinding media for effective particle size reduction. Disc mills employ a rotating mechanism and stationary disks to crush materials. Rotor mills feature high-speed rotors for rapid processing. Cutting mills use sharp blades to slice through samples, ensuring uniform size. Each type serves distinct applications in research, materials testing, and quality control across various industries, catering to diverse milling needs.</p></p>
