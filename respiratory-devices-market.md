<p><p>The respiratory devices market is categorized into invasive and non-invasive types based on their method of delivering therapy. Invasive devices, such as ventilators and tracheostomy tubes, require surgical intervention and are typically used for patients with severe respiratory conditions, providing direct airway access. Non-invasive devices, such as CPAP machines and nebulizers, offer support without the need for surgery, making them ideal for managing less critical respiratory issues. Both types cater to diverse patient needs across various clinical settings.</p></p>
