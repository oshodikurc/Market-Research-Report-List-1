<p><ul><li>Stump Grinding</li><li>Stump Removal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stump services encompass two primary market types: stump grinding and stump removal. Stump grinding involves using specialized machinery to grind down tree stumps to below ground level, allowing for faster site restoration and minimal disruption to the surrounding area. In contrast, stump removal entails extracting the entire stump, including its root system, which can be more labor-intensive and costly but provides a clean slate for landscaping. Both services cater to residential and commercial clients seeking to enhance property aesthetics and safety.</p></p>
