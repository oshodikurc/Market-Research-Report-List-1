<p><p>The internal radiotherapy devices market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on HDR (High Dose Rate) and PDR ( pulsed Dose Rate) afterloaders. HDR afterloaders deliver high doses of radiation in short bursts, minimizing exposure time and allowing for precise targeting of tumors while sparing surrounding healthy tissue. In contrast, PDR afterloaders administer radiation in smaller, intermittent pulses over an extended period, promoting tissue recovery and reducing side effects. Both technologies enhance treatment effectiveness while improving patient safety and comfort.</p></p>
