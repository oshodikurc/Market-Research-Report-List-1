<p><strong>The SMT Inspection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automated X-ray Inspection</li><li>Automated Optical Inspection</li><li>Solder Paste Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SMT inspection systems encompass several key types that ensure quality in electronics manufacturing. Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI) provides detailed internal views of components, detecting hidden faults like voids in solder joints. Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) employs high-resolution cameras to visually inspect surface-mounted devices for defects such as misalignments or missing parts. Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) focuses on the quality of solder paste application, measuring volume and placement accuracy before components are placed on printed circuit boards, ensuring optimal soldering outcomes.</p></p>
