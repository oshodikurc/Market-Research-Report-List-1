<p><strong>The Alumina Ceramic Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microfiltration</li><li>Ultrafiltration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alumina ceramic membrane market is categorized into microfiltration and ultrafiltration types. Microfiltration membranes have larger pore sizes, typically around 0.1 to 10 micrometers, effectively removing suspended solids, bacteria, and larger particles from liquids. Ultrafiltration membranes feature smaller pores, ranging from 0.01 to 0.1 micrometers, allowing for the separation of macromolecules, colloids, and some viruses. Both types offer high chemical resistance, durability, and effective filtration, making them suitable for various applications in water treatment, food processing, and biotechnology.</p></p>
