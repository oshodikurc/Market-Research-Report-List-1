<p><p>Moisture curing polyurethane adhesives are available in two primary types: liquid and hot melt. Liquid polyurethane adhesives offer excellent bonding strength and flexibility, making them ideal for various applications, such as woodworking and construction. They cure by absorbing moisture from the air, leading to durable bonds. Hot melt polyurethane adhesives, on the other hand, are thermoplastic and are applied in a molten state, quickly solidifying upon cooling. They provide fast bonding and are commonly used in packaging and assembly applications.</p></p>
