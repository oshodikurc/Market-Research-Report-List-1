<p><p>The Specialty Absorptive Dressings market encompasses two main types: Non-Adhering Dressings and Self-Adhering Dressings. Non-Adhering Dressings are designed to prevent adhesion to the wound bed, minimizing pain during dressing changes and protecting delicate tissue. Conversely, Self-Adhering Dressings utilize adhesive properties to securely adhere to the surrounding skin, providing stability and effective absorption of exudate. Both types play crucial roles in managing various wound conditions, enhancing healing, and improving patient comfort.</p></p>
