<p><ul><li>Optical Sensing</li><li>GMR Sensor Proximity Detection</li><li>Physical Attributes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The currency detector market comprises various types, including optical sensing, GMR sensor proximity detection, and physical attributes evaluation. Optical sensing utilizes advanced imaging techniques to identify counterfeit currency through visual patterns and features. GMR sensor proximity detection employs magnetoresistive technology to sense magnetic properties unique to legitimate banknotes. Meanwhile, analyzing physical attributes involves evaluating tactile and dimensional characteristics, such as texture and size, to differentiate authentic currency from counterfeits. Each method enhances security and confidence in currency transactions.</p></p>
