<p><p>The biodegradable cup market is primarily divided into two types: paper-coated products and thermoforming products. Paper-coated products typically consist of a paper base coated with a biodegradable lining, making them suitable for hot and cold beverages while ensuring compostability. On the other hand, thermoforming products are made from biodegradable plastics that are shaped through heating and molding processes, offering a flexible and durable option for various applications. Both types cater to the growing demand for sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
