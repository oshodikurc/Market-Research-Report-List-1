<p><strong>The Cardiovascular Disease Diagnostic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In Vitro Diagnostics</li><li>In Vivo Diagnostics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiovascular disease diagnostic equipment market can be categorized into two main types: In Vitro Diagnostics and In Vivo Diagnostics. In Vitro Diagnostics encompasses tests performed on samples taken from the body, such as blood or urine, to detect biomarkers, cholesterol levels, and other indicators of heart health. In Vivo Diagnostics involves real-time monitoring and imaging techniques, such as echocardiography and angiography, to assess heart function and structure directly within the body. Both types are essential for early detection and management of cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
