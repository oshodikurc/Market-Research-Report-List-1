<p><p>Von Willebrand Disease (VWD) is a hereditary bleeding disorder caused by a deficiency or dysfunction of von Willebrand factor, which is crucial for platelet adhesion and blood clotting. Treatment modalities primarily include desmopressin, which stimulates the release of von Willebrand factor, and replacement therapies such as clotting factor concentrates. In severe cases, patients may require antifibrinolytic agents to minimize bleeding.</p><p>The Von Willebrand Disease Treatment Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 19.00% during the forecast period, driven by increasing awareness of the disorder, advancements in treatment options, and a greater focus on personalized medicine.
